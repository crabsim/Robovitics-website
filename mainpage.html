About Us

How we started ?

It was the passion of our seniors Kumar Utkarsh (B.Tech EEE, 2007-2011) and Ayush Agarwal (B.Tech EIE 2008-2012) that laid the milestone for our club. With great enthusiastic spirits, RoboVITics was founded on 22nd October, 2010 in VIT University, Vellore. Our club turned out to be the first technical club in VIT providing a platform for all tech people to make their desired projects with the support and guidance of our club members.

Our Mission

The prime concern of the club is to introduce and spread technical knowledge to every student possible. Apart from participating in events, workshops and competitions, members of RoboVITics bear a responsibility to pass on their technical knowledge to all those interested people who are in the quest of searching for technical stuff. We are also indulged in several social outreach programmes, where we go and teach the children of various underprivileged schools about science, robotics and electronics

What we Do

Quite simple to say, the prime objective of RoboVITics is to spread technical knowledge to students in and around the radius of VIT. Robotics is now a budding technological field, and it should be reachable to everyone.
